What We Do

Here at Better Days, we understand the struggles many men endure during their life.
Our belief is that many categories of men are being underrepresented and as a result, resources can be hard to come by.
Our quiz questions are person specific, based off your profile we provide you relevant and adaptive
questions to allow us to provide the best possible resources to aid your treatment.

Age

Studies show that men from different age ranges deal with separate battles. Our diagnosis and treatment reflects this.

Sexuality

Studies show that men with different age sexual orientations deal with separate battles. Our diagnosis and treatment reflects this

Substance Abuse

Mental Health issues can often lead to substance abuse. Our aim is to provide the appropriate resources to aid the treatment of both

Accessibility

Mental Health affects people of all ages and abilities. We have created an easy to use app for all levels of ability.
